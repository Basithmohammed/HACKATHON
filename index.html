About The Event

About The Event

CIIdeate 2024 is an exciting one month coding marathon designed to bring together developers, designers, and entrepreneurs from various backgrounds to collaborate on innovative projects. The event aims to foster creativity, teamwork, and problem-solving skills in a high-energy environment.
